Hello.

Hi , I'm Caroline. I'm a front-end developer based in Toronto who has been cultivating what some may call a "serious" relationship with JavaScript, while also fostering a friendship with Google at the same time. While I can problem solve independently, I love nothing more than solving problems with others. Mob and pair programming are some of my favourite parts of web development because it gives me the opportunity to learn from others while building new products, features, and squashing bugs.

As a child, I enjoyed building websites by peeling back the source code and fusing together whatever I could. This led me down a path of teaching myself Photoshop at a very young age just so I could design graphics to support my code. During the Northeast blackout of 2003 I lost a weeks worth of unsaved work and learned a hard lesson at a very young age: "commit early, commit often". I'm now going forth learning as much as I can, maintaining the same curiosity as my 13 year old self, while remembering to hit "Save".
